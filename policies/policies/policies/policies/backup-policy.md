# Backup Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the requirements for backing up business-critical data and systems.

## Policy Statement
- Critical business data must be backed up regularly.
- Backups must be tested periodically.
- Backup access must be restricted to authorized personnel.
- Backup copies should be protected against unauthorized access and data loss.

## Backup Frequency
- Daily backups for critical operational data
- Weekly backups for less critical systems
- Monthly archival backups where required

## Retention
Backup retention periods should be defined according to business, legal, and operational needs.

## Review
Backup processes should be reviewed regularly to confirm they remain effective.
