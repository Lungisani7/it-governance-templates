# Password Policy

## Purpose
This policy establishes minimum password requirements to protect company systems and data.

## Password Requirements
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
- Passwords must contain a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters where supported.
- Passwords must not be easily guessable.
- Passwords must not be shared with any other person.
- Default or temporary passwords must be changed immediately.

## Multi-Factor Authentication
Multi-factor authentication should be enabled where available, especially for email, cloud services, and administrative accounts.

## Password Storage
Passwords must not be written down openly or stored in insecure documents.

## Non-Compliance
Failure to follow password requirements may result in account restrictions or disciplinary action.
