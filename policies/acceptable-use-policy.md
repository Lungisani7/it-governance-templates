# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines acceptable use of company information technology resources.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who use company systems, devices, networks, or data.

## Policy Rules
- Company devices and systems must be used primarily for business purposes.
- Users must not install unauthorized software.
- Users must not share passwords.
- Users must not access, download, or distribute inappropriate or unlawful content.
- Users must report suspected security incidents immediately.
- Company data must not be copied to unauthorized personal devices or accounts.

## Monitoring
The organization reserves the right to monitor the use of its systems, devices, and networks where permitted by law and company policy.

## Non-Compliance
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action.
